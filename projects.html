Forecasting International Tourism with R
Tools: R, ggplot2, forecast, tseries, tidyverse

I conducted time series analysis to forecast international tourism arrivals in Europe & Central Asia, analyzing the economic impact of COVID-19. Models used include ETS (e.g., Winters method), ARIMA, moving average, and linear regression. I also performed residual diagnostics, stationarity checks (ADF test), and compared model accuracy using MAPE, MAD, MSD, and AIC.

Automating Telco Fault Alerts with Workato
Tools: Workato, Google Sheets, Salesforce, GenAI, Telegram

Built a GenAI-integrated customer support automation pipeline as part of a telco solution. This included ingesting fault data from Amdocs, storing it in a data lake (Google Sheets), generating alerts via GenAI, and pushing notifications to users through Telegram. Tools: UiPath, JoyForm, Google Sheets, Tableau, Asana
